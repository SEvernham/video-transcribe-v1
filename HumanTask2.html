Notes

Detailed Subtitle Review Instructions

1. Subtitle Format

  • Each subtitle entry consists of three parts:
    1. Subtitle number (sequential)
    2. Timestamp (00:00:00,000 --> 00:00:00,000)
    3. Text content (one or more lines)
  • Leave a blank line between subtitle entries
  • Don't modify timestamp format

2. Text Guidelines

  • Maximum 2 lines per subtitle
  • Maximum 32 characters per line
  • Minimum duration: 1 second
  • Maximum duration: 7 seconds
  • Use proper capitalization and punctuation

3. Common Issues to Watch For

  • Timing misalignment with speech
  • Line breaks in inappropriate places
  • Overlapping timestamps
  • Missing speaker identification if needed
  • Technical terms and proper nouns

4. Quality Ratings Explained

  • High Quality:
    • Accurate transcription
    • Proper timing
    • Correct formatting
    • Only minor adjustments needed
  • Medium Quality:
    • Some timing issues
    • Some formatting problems
    • Multiple text corrections needed
  • Low Quality:
    • Major timing problems
    • Significant text errors
    • Poor formatting
    • Requires extensive editing
